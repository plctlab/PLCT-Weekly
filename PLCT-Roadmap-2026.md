# There is no PLCT Roadmap 2026

The PLCT Lab is affiliated with the Intelligent Software Research Center (ISRC) of the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS). According to a unified brand adjustment by the superior department, starting from October 17, 2025, the branding of various sub-teams, including the PLCT Lab, will be gradually phased out, and the focus will shift to highlighting brands such as RuyiSDK and other RISC-V development toolkits.

- The monthly progress summaries of RISC-V will continue to be updated.
- The annual wish list will no longer be maintained. Please refer to the Jia-Chen Project wish list (if available).
- The annual PLCT roadmap will no longer be published. Please refer to the Jia-Chen Project roadmap (if available).
- The original PLCT OpenDay event will no longer be held. Weekly intern presentations will continue to be uploaded to Bilibili.

Welcome to the RuyiSDK community at https://RuyiSDK.org and https://RuyiSDK.cn!

# 关于PLCT实验室品牌调整的提示

PLCT实验室隶属于中国科学院软件研究所（ISCAS）智能软件研究中心（ISRC）。根据上级部门的统一品牌调整，自2025年10月17日起，将会逐步淡化包括PLCT实验室在内的各个子团队的品牌，转而突出RuyiSDK等RISC-V开发工具套件品牌。

- PLCT实验室的每月进展汇总依然保持更新。
- 每年的许愿清单不再进行。欢迎去甲辰计划许愿（如果有的话）。
- 每年的PLCT路线图不再公布。欢迎去甲辰计划路线图参考（如果有的话）。
- 原来的PLCT OpenDay 活动不再进行。实习生每周分享依然会在B站上传。

欢迎关注 https://RuyiSDK.org 和 https://RuyiSDK.cn 社区！
